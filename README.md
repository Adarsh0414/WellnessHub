# Wellness Hub

Welcome to **Wellness Hub**, a user-friendly website dedicated to promoting health and wellness through easy-to-use tools and expert resources.
Wellness Hub is a comprehensive health and wellness website designed to empower users to take charge of their well-being. It features tools like BMI Check for quick health assessments, expert insights on Nutrition and Lifestyle for healthy living, and a curated list of experienced doctors available for consultation. Users can easily connect with healthcare professionals via email, making Wellness Hub a convenient, all-in-one resource for personalized preventive care and lifestyle management.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the BMI Check tool to assess your health using simple metrics.
2. Explore the Nutrition and Lifestyle pages for educational content to enhance your daily habits.
3. Find a suitable doctor from the list and contact them via the provided email addresses for expert advice.
