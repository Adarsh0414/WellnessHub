How BMI is categorized

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: 30 or higher

We also estimate daily calorie needs using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation and suggest a target range based on your goal implied by category.
